About us

Kilimanjaro, a leading brand under the Sundry Foods umbrella, is redefining the food online delivery experience within the Quick Service Restaurant (QSR) industry. As one of the fastest-growing restaurant brands, Kilimanjaro proudly operates 84 stores across 17 states and is continually expanding to serve more customers through convenient online food delivery services nationwide.

Dedicated to catering to diverse culinary tastes in sub-Saharan Africa, Kilimanjaro offers a distinctive menu filled with contemporary and authentic Nigerian dishes, enjoyed by households across Africa. With our commitment to quality and freshness, Kilimanjaro provides a seamless food online delivery option, allowing customers to enjoy their favorite meals pastries, sandwiches, and beverages from the comfort of their homes. Our restaurants are known for their vibrant decor and inviting ambiance, but we’re equally dedicated to delivering the same quality experience through online food delivery.

Our vision is to open a Kilimanjaro restaurant in every major city, making it easier than ever to enjoy our delicious offerings through food online delivery or a visit to one of our welcoming locations.
